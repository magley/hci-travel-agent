Arrangement Search and Filtering

To aid you in searching amongst hunderds of arrangements, YouTravel provides a flexible mechanism for filtering out arrangements to your liking.

Image 1. shows all the controls for searching and filtering.
Enter any text in the texbox, hit the search button, and we will return arrangements with a matching name.
YouTravel also supports fine-grained filtering:

  • Active toggles arrangements whose trips are currently in progress.
  • Upcoming toggles arrangements that are yet to begin.
  • Finished toggles arrangements that are completed.
That way, if you want to view or modify arrangements which clients can still book, YouTravel offers you to do so.
