Place

A place is a location with a name that arrangements can list as part of their activities. One arrangement can have many places. One place can be a part of many arrangements.

A place can be any of the following:

  • A hotel, where our clients can rest in
  • A restaurant , where they can eat, or
  • A tourist attraction, which the clients can visit as part of the travel arrangement.

A place is described by its name, description, location and type (one of the 3 mentioned above).

Image 1 shows the main screen for places. This is where you can get an overview of all places, with support for searching and filtering.
You can open this screen at any time by clicking on the View Places toolbar button, opening View > Places, or hitting the CTRL + P hotkey.

When you hover over any place in the list, buttons for removing, and editing the place will become visible.

You can add a new place by double-clicking on the map.
